About Us

Allen's Bakery was opened in 2009. We currently have 4 locations in Orlando.
We only use the freshest ingredients bought locally. We've been happy to serve
you over the past 8 years and look forward to continue doing so.
We only use vegan ingredients for all our products, from the donuts
to the cheesecakes.
